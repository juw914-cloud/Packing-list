This is the one list that controls everything — every field is editable. Click an item’s name to rename it; change its weight, quantity or battery; tap carried by to set which appointments carry it; or press the red ✗ Strike off button to strike it off — it stays here in the Master List (crossed out, so you never lose it) but is removed from the packing lists and the shared view, and out of the weight total. The button turns into ↻ Restore to bring it back. You can also strike a single detail line the same way. Each item also has a ↺ Reset to put just that one item back to its Masterlist default. Every item’s inspection detail from the Inspection Masterlist is shown right underneath it — click any line to edit the wording, press Enter for a new line, + add detail line to add one, or hit the ✗ on any single line to strike off just that line (it stays, crossed out). To read faster, tap any section header to collapse it, or use Collapse sections / Hide detail in the toolbar. The General Packing List and every establishment are previews that update from here automatically. Batteries start at 100% = the issued fit (e.g. PRC650 = 6 × AA); use the Batt fit − / + to step to 200% (12 × AA, double weight), 300%, and so on. Nothing resets unless you choose to: ↺ resets one item, Clear all strike-offs restores every crossed-out item, and Reset all to baseline starts the whole list over.
